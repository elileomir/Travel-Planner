## 🚗 TRAVEL TIPS

### Driving Tips
1. **Early departure recommended** - Leave San Pedro 1AM to avoid Metro Manila traffic
2. **TPLEX route** - Faster than NLEX-SCTEx for La Union
3. **Rest stops** - Plan breakfast/bathroom breaks at TPLEX rest areas
4. **Mountain roads** - La Union to Baguio has winding roads, drive carefully
5. **Parking** - Most spots in Baguio have limited parking, go early

### Packing Essentials
**La Union (Beach):**
- Swimwear, rash guard, beach towel
- Sunscreen SPF 50+, sunglasses, hat
- Waterproof phone case/bag
- Reef-safe sunscreen for ocean
- Flip-flops and water shoes

**Baguio (Mountain):**
- Light jacket/hoodie (temperature 14-27°C)
- Comfortable walking shoes (lots of walking!)
- Umbrella or light raincoat
- Reusable bag for shopping/pasalubong
- Closed-toe shoes for strawberry farm

**General:**
- Power bank, chargers
- Camera/smartphone
- Cash (many small vendors don't accept cards)
- Valid IDs
- Reusable water bottle

### Money-Saving Tips
1. **Eat at carinderias** - ₱60-120 meals vs ₱200+ at restaurants
2. **Shop at night market** - Cheapest thrift finds
3. **Buy pasalubong at Good Shepherd** - Direct from source, fresher
4. **Use jeepneys** - ₱10-20 vs ₱100-200 taxi
5. **Surfboard rental only** - ₱200/hour if you can surf already
6. **Free attractions** - Burnham Park, beaches, viewpoints, parks
7. **Travel early** - Better parking, less crowds, cooler weather

### Best Times to Visit Places
- **Strawberry Farm:** Early morning (7-9AM) for freshest picks
- **Mines View Park:** Early morning or late afternoon (clearer views)
- **Night Market:** 10PM-12AM (peak selection, energy)
- **Beaches:** Afternoon for sunset (5-6PM)
- **Restaurants:** Avoid lunch rush 12-1PM and dinner rush 7-8PM

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES

### Health & Safety
1. **Altitude:** Baguio is at high elevation, some may feel lightheaded initially
2. **Cold weather:** Temperature can drop to 14°C at night in Baguio
3. **Sun protection:** UV index 5-6 in La Union, use sunscreen
4. **Water safety:** Don't swim if red flag is up at beaches
5. **Food safety:** Eat at busy establishments, avoid empty restaurants

### Practical Reminders
1. **Strawberry products** - Need refrigeration, 1-month shelf life
2. **Night market** - Opens 9PM, bring small bills for easier transactions
3. **Good Shepherd** - Can get crowded on weekends, go early
4. **Tangadan Falls** - Mandatory guide, slippery rocks, wear proper footwear
5. **BenCab Museum** - Closed Mondays, last entry 5:30PM
6. **Bell Church** - No photos inside temple, respect the rules
