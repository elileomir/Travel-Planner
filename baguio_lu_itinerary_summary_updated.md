## Structure by day

### Day 1 – Thu Mar 19 (Baguio intro + ukay)
- Early morning drive from San Pedro → Baguio.
- Session Road warm‑up walk and early lunch at **Good Taste**.
- Check‑in and rest at your Baguio stay.
- **Baguio Cathedral + Session Road** walk (FREE).
- **Burnham Park** stroll and optional boating/biking (₱100/person for activities).
- Dinner at **Farmer's Daughter** (Cordilleran food) near Tam‑awan (₱350/person).
- **Baguio Night Market (Harrison Road)** for ukay + street food (₱500/person budget).

### Day 2 – Fri Mar 20 (Strawberries + museums + views)
- Breakfast near your accommodation.
- **La Trinidad Strawberry Farm**: picking + pasalubong (₱400/person).
- **BenCab Museum** entrance ₱150 + coffee/lunch at **Cafe Sabel** (₱500/person).
- **Tam‑awan Village** (₱60 entrance) art and viewpoints.
- **Mines View Park** (₱10 entrance) souvenirs and snacks.
- Dinner at **50s Diner**, then optional Baguio cafe hopping.

### Day 3 – Sat Mar 21 (Finish Baguio, move to Elyu)
- Breakfast at a local carinderia.
- **Baguio Botanical Garden** (₱150 entrance for tourists).
- **The Mansion + Wright Park** walk (FREE, optional horse ride extra).
- Lunch at **Cafe by the Ruins** or similar (₱550/person).
- Daytime ukay at **Bayanihan / Sky World**.
- **Drive Baguio → San Juan, La Union** (~1.5–2.5 hours depending on traffic).
- Check‑in at your Elyu stay and **sunset walk on Urbiztondo Beach**.
- Budget dinner at **Tagpuan** or similar rice‑bowl place.
- Optional bars/cafe hopping along the Urbiztondo strip.

### Day 4 – Sun Mar 22 (Surf + beach + food)
- Breakfast near your Urbiztondo accommodation.
- **Beginner surf lesson** at the San Juan surf area (₱650/hour - board + instructor).
- Free swim and chill at **Urbiztondo Beach**.
- Brunch/early lunch at budget spots in San Juan.
- Coffee/merienda at **El Union Coffee** or **Makai Bowls** in **The Great Northwest**.
- Rest at accommodation.
- Sunset beach walk + light street food.
- Dinner at another budget Elyu resto; optional drinks or early rest.

### Day 5 – Mon Mar 23 (Extra surf / Elyu chill day)
- Light breakfast.
- Free morning: 2nd surf session (₱650) or just relax on the beach.
- Early lunch/brunch at **San Juan food spots**.
- Afternoon cafe/chill at **The Great Northwest** (El Union/Makai, etc.).
- Beach chill and sunset walk at Urbiztondo.
- Mid‑range dinner at **Kermit La Union** or similar.
- Drinks / social night or rest around Urbiztondo.

### Day 6 – Tue Mar 24 (Tangadan Falls)
- Breakfast near accommodation.
- Drive **San Juan → San Gabriel** (Tangadan jump‑off).
- **Trek + swim at Tangadan Falls** with a local guide (₱30 entrance + ₱250 guide fee per person when split for 2).
- Late lunch in San Gabriel or back in San Juan.
- Drive back to San Juan and rest at your accommodation.
- Final full sunset walk at Urbiztondo + street food.
- Simple dinner at **Tagpuan / Elyu food strip**, plus packing for checkout.

### Day 7 – Wed Mar 25 (Elyu → Home)
- Breakfast & check‑out at your La Union accommodation.
- **Drive San Juan, La Union → San Pedro / South Luzon** via TPLEX/SCTEX/NLEX/SLEX (6–7 hours with stops).
